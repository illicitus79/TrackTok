How to use TrackTok

A quick walkthrough of the core flow and how to read the analytics on your dashboard.

1
Set your default currency

Visit Settings and pick the tenant currency. All accounts, projects, and expenses will use it.

↓
2
Create an account

Accounts hold balances (cash, card, bank). Create your first account to fund projects and expenses.

Create Account
↓
3
Create a project

Define the workstream, choose the source account, and (optionally) set a budget. Projects group related expenses.

Go to Projects
↓
4
Log expenses

Add expenses to the project. Attach receipts, choose categories, and keep amounts consistent with your project currency.

Add Expense
A
Reading your analytics

Dashboard hero: Month-to-date spend, active projects, and the latest expense give you a fast pulse.

Project dashboard: See utilization, remaining budget, top vendors, and alerts together for each active project.

Health by budget: For each active project, see utilization and remaining budget to avoid overspend.

Expense velocity: Tracks how many expenses you're logging and average ticket size.

Activity feed: Shows recent vendors, notes, and categories so you can spot anomalies quickly.

T
Tips

- Keep one account per real funding source.
- Use projects to isolate budgets; the same account can fund multiple projects if needed.
- Categories make analytics richer; add them as you go.
- Attach receipts for faster reviews and audits.

- Visit Settings to set currency, timezone, and date format so dashboards and alerts stay consistent.
